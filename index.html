WE DEVELOP SUSTAINABLE IOT BASED SOLUTIONS

Smart Sensing device for coconut tree pest detection

WeeVibes enables early and accurate detection of Asiatic Palm Weevil larvae in coconut trees. It provides real-time alerts through a web-based interface for continuous monitoring. By detecting infestations early, it helps prevent damage and supports effective pest management.

Its smart sensing technology enhances pest detection, allowing farmers to respond promptly and protect their coconut trees.

Features

Status

Get real-time status immediately upon larvae detection.

Detection

Vibration detection for weevil larvae inside the coconut trunk.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We’re here to answer! If you don’t see your question here, feel free to contact us!

What is Weevibes?

Weevibes is an Asiatic Palm Weevil larvae detection device designed to detect the presence of harmful weevils in coconut palms to prevent damage and improve crop health.

How do I create an account on WeeVibes?

To get started, simply click on the Sign Up button on the homepage. Fill in your details, create a username and password, and you're all set! You can now be a protector of our coconut trees.

How do I use the WeeVibes device?

Attach the device to the coconut tree trunk, power it on, then connect your phone or laptop. You can now start monitoring real-time data to help protect the tree.
